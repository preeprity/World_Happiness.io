Country wise analysis of Happiness scores

Study

The map shows the world map color coded as per their happiness score and happiness rank. The countries with good score are indicated by Green and as score reduces, the color changes to lighter shades of green further into shades of Pink.

Hovering over the countries opens a pop up with the details of country name, Happines Score and Rank.

As of 2017, Norway is the happiest country with score of about 7.54. Central African Republic is least happy with lowest score of 2.96 and lowest rank 155.

On the whole, Australia , Europe, North America and South America are generally in the happier belt. Europe is one continent with overall happiness score above 5.5. But we see the exceptions are Ukraine, Albania and Bulagria (In pink) with scores less than 5.

Countries of Africa, South Asia fall under not very happy bucket. Almost whole of Africa has score less than 5.Exceptions are Algeria and Libya with the score of 5.87 and 5.53 resp.
